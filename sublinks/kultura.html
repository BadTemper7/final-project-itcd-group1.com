Pagbabatok is a traditional tattooing technique practiced by various indigenous tribes in the Philippines. It involves the intricate process of hand-tapping ink into the skin using thorns, bamboo sticks, or other sharp tools.

Maranao architecture is characterized by the grand and elaborate structures known as "torogan." These traditional houses are typically made of wood, featuring intricate carvings and designs. Torogans serve as symbols of prestige and are often used for social gatherings and ceremonies.

Sinulog is a vibrant and festive celebration held annually in Cebu City, Philippines.
