Brassware Making

The Maranao people of Mindanao are known for their intricate brassware, which are often used for traditional rituals and ceremonies. The craft has been passed down through generations and involves a long and meticulous process of casting, engraving, and polishing. The brassware is highly prized and is considered a symbol of wealth and status in Maranao society.
