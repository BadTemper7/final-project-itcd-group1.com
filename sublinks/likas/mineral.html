Yamang Mineral

Natural resources that we can find in our nation include mineral resources. The earth's interior can be seen. There are three different sorts of mineral resources, but they cannot be substituted or augmented. It consists of metal, non-metal, and fuel. Mineral resources are easily depleted because they cannot be replaced. In addition, additional areas of mother nature are being destroyed in order to extract it owing to the illegal operations of large corporations. Although mining produces a lot of money, the reward is the devastation of mother nature. As a result, not only the humans but also the forest's wildlife are impacted.
